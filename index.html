About me

Hi there! My name is Tarah Perry (AKA: Tarah Dactyl) and I'm a web developer, cave geek, and outdoor enthusiast. I love to build (web applications, tables, legos, fires, etc), and I love learning how to build better. I graduated from Southwestern Unversity in May 2013 with a B.S. in Geo Physics. I grew up just east of Austin, TX, and have an affinity for tiny home livin', food trucks, and live music. Away from the desk chair I can be found hiking, bouldering at the gym, at my favorite neighborhood pub, or making myself some delicious Tom Kha.
